    XDMarket Design¶

    XDMarket is a Mozilla project to explore the use of Blockchain, Incentive Algorithms and Prediction Markets as tooling for Community Managed Software.

    This site has use-cases and wireframes for areas where we want to examine & discuss design ideas.
